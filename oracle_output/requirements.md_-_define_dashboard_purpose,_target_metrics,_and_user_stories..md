# Dashboard Requirements

## Purpose
A centralized analytics dashboard providing real-time visibility into key business performance indicators, enabling data-driven decision making and proactive issue identification.

## Target Metrics

### Core Performance Metrics
- **Revenue**: Daily/Monthly/Quarterly revenue trends
- **User Engagement**: Active users, session duration, feature adoption
- **Conversion Rate**: Visitor-to-customer conversion percentages
- **Customer Satisfaction**: NPS score, CSAT ratings, support ticket volume

### Operational Metrics
- **System Health**: API response times, error rates, uptime percentage
- **Sales Pipeline**: Lead volume, conversion stages, sales cycle length
- **Support Efficiency**: First response time, resolution rate, ticket backlog

### Business Health Metrics
- **Customer Retention**: Churn rate, renewal percentages
- **Cost Metrics**: Customer acquisition cost, operational expenses
- **Growth Indicators**: New customer acquisition, market share trends

## User Stories

### Executive Stakeholders
- As a CEO, I want to see overall business health at a glance to make strategic decisions
- As a CTO, I need to monitor system performance and technical debt metrics to prioritize engineering efforts

### Department Managers
- As a Sales Director, I want to track pipeline progress and team performance against quotas
- As a Marketing Manager, I need to measure campaign ROI and lead generation effectiveness
- As a Support Manager, I want to monitor team workload and customer satisfaction trends

### Individual Contributors
- As a Sales Representative, I need to see my personal performance metrics and upcoming priorities
- As a Customer Support Agent, I want quick access to ticket volume and resolution statistics
- As a Product Manager, I need to understand feature usage patterns to guide development priorities

### Data/Operations Teams
- As a Data Analyst, I require access to raw metrics for deeper investigation and reporting
- As a System Administrator, I need real-time alerts for critical system failures or performance degradation

## Success Criteria
- 90% reduction in time spent gathering metrics from multiple sources
- All critical business decisions supported by dashboard data within 3 months
- User satisfaction score of 4.5/5 or higher from primary stakeholders
- Automated alerts for all metric thresholds breached, with zero false positives
